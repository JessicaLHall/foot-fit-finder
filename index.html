How to Measure Length
Stand on a piece of paper and mark the back of your heel and the tip of your longest toe. Measure between the marks in mm. Measure both feet and use the longer one.
How to Measure Width
Stand on a piece of paper and mark the widest points on each side of your foot across the ball. Measure between the marks in mm. Do this standing — feet spread more under load.
Thumb Space
Running shoes should have 10–15 mm of space between your longest toe and the end of the shoe. This allows for foot swelling and toe splay during a run.
